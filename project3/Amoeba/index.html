DARK KINGDOM ARC

Usagi Tsukino, a 14-year-old girl, is confronted by a talking black cat named Luna, who reveals to her that she is a warrior from an ancient kingdom, whose duty it is to find her allies and save her princess.

BLACK MOON ARC

In the Black Moon arc of Sailor Moon, the Ghost of Death, an ancient criminal from Earth, disguises himself as the "Great Sage" to deceive the members of Black Moon and the Sailor Senshi.

MUGEN ARC

Strange hunches about the end of the world are detected by Mamoru and Rei as monsters begin to attack students around Mugen College.

YUME ARC

During a solar eclipse, a strange ship-shaped craft lands in Japan, but the only ones who notice it are Usagi, Chibiusa, and Mamoru.

STARS ARC

Mamoru receives a scholarship to study abroad and when he is about to get on the plane a figure appears and steals his Crystal Sailor. A new enemy appears and at the same time three new sailors.
